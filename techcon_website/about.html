Our History

TechCon was founded in 2015 as a platform for technologists, innovators, and entrepreneurs to share ideas, collaborate, and learn from one another. Over the years, it has grown into a global movement, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by providing a space where creativity, diversity, and innovation thrive. We are committed to fostering inclusion, education, and meaningful dialogue in the tech industry.
